# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We, as contributors and maintainers of this multi‑tier repository, pledge to foster a respectful, harassment‑free environment across all layers of the project — whether front‑end, back‑end, infrastructure, or documentation.  
We welcome individuals of all backgrounds and identities, and commit to building an inclusive, collaborative community.

---

## Our Standards

**Positive behaviors include:**
- Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward others  
- Respecting differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences  
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback  
- Documenting changes clearly so all tiers (UI, API, infra, docs) remain aligned  
- Focusing on what is best for the overall project and community  

**Unacceptable behaviors include:**
- Sexualized language, imagery, or attention  
- Trolling, insults, or personal/political attacks  
- Public or private harassment  
- Publishing private information without consent  
- Any conduct inappropriate in a professional setting  

---

## Responsibilities
Project leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards.  
They may remove, edit, or reject contributions not aligned with this Code of Conduct and will explain moderation decisions when appropriate.

---

## Scope
This Code applies to all project spaces (issues, pull requests, commits, discussions, docs) and when representing the project publicly (e.g., social media, conferences).

---

## Enforcement
Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers at **quietopscb@gmail.com**.  
All complaints will be reviewed promptly and fairly, with respect for the privacy of the reporter.

---

## Enforcement Guidelines

### 1. Correction
**Impact**: Inappropriate language or behavior.  
**Consequence**: Private written warning; public apology may be requested.  

### 2. Warning
**Impact**: Single incident or series of violations.  
**Consequence**: Formal warning; limited interaction for a set period.  

### 3. Temporary Ban
**Impact**: Serious or sustained inappropriate behavior.  
**Consequence**: Temporary ban from community interaction.  

### 4. Permanent Ban
**Impact**: Repeated violations, harassment, or aggression.  
**Consequence**: Permanent removal from community participation.
