Reporting a Vulnerability
We take security seriously and appreciate responsible disclosure.
- How to report:
Please email quietopscb@gmail.com with the subject line: Security Vulnerability Report.
- What to include:
- A clear description of the vulnerability
- Steps to reproduce (if applicable)
- Potential impact and affected versions
- Any suggested fixes or mitigations
- Response expectations:
- You will receive an acknowledgment within 72 hours.
- We will provide status updates at least every 7 days until resolution.
- If the vulnerability is accepted, we will work on a fix and credit the reporter (if desired).
- If declined, we will explain why (e.g., not reproducible, out of scope, or already fixed).
- Confidentiality:
Please do not disclose the vulnerability publicly until we have confirmed and released a fix. Coordinated disclosure helps protect all users.

Commitment
Our goal is to maintain a secure troubleshooting playground that reflects professional standards. By reporting responsibly, you help strengthen the reliability and trustworthiness of this project.
